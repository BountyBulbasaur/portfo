About Me

My story and my goals.

The Journey of Daniel Cervantes

My name is Daniel and I began my journey into Web3 security by starting with Web2 security. I learned python in Kali Linux when I was learning Ethical Hacking by writing scripts that would help automate the process. I was able to make backdoors, keyloggers, credential harvesters, network hacking tools and website hacking tools. However, after diving into the Web2 space I found the Web3 space and decided I wanted to change my goals a little.

I still wanted to work in cybersecurity, but I wanted to focus in Web3 because there seems to be a growing demand. I started following bitcoin in 2012, which later lead me to Ethereum. I started reading the whitepapers of some projects and started to really understand the possibilities with blockchain. I was hooked.

I started reading as many books as I can find about Blockchain, Ethereum, and DeFi. I even started looking into NFTs, but I was more interested in the finance side of the space. I joined the Chainlink hackathon 2022 so I can get a great amount of useful information and I have no regrets. I needed to wake up at 4:20 AM for most days to attend the classes live, but the excitement was enough to keep me up.

Currently, I am still working on my project for the hackathon, but I have also focused a lot of my time in completing some capture the flags, and watching the free YouTube videos on Secureum's channel. From this, I was able to get some helpful auditing tools that I was able to use on my own Smart Contracts. Although I am still new to the space, I really hope to dive deeper to learn from all the top auditing companies and contribute to the space once I build my knowledge. Until then, I will rely on my blog to update my progress.
